Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

Flick Kick Football does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Flick Kick Football is a completely offline, single-player game. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data, user information, or device identifiers.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

No Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising SDKs (no ads)
  • Analytics frameworks
  • Social media integrations
  • In-app purchase systems
  • Crash reporting tools

Children's Privacy

Flick Kick Football is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Since no data is collected at all, this app is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

Data Security

All game data (progress, scores, settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data is protected by the built-in security features of iOS. No data is ever transmitted over the internet or stored on external servers.

Data Retention

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data retention concern. All game progress exists solely on your device and can be removed at any time by deleting the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our App Store page.
