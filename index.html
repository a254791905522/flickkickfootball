About the Game

Flick Kick Football puts you in the boots of a free kick specialist. Master the art of swiping to control your shot's power, direction, and spin as you curve the ball past defensive walls and outsmart agile goalkeepers.

With 50 carefully designed levels across 5 difficulty tiers, every free kick is a unique puzzle. Early levels teach you the basics, while later stages introduce wind effects, taller walls, and lightning-fast keepers that demand pinpoint accuracy.

Earn up to 3 stars per level based on your performance. Can you achieve a perfect score across all 50 challenges? Whether you have 30 seconds or 30 minutes, Flick Kick Football delivers satisfying, pick-up-and-play football action.
